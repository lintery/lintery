### Product-first backend engineer

I am **Mikhail Mironov**, a **20-year-old Middle Backend Engineer** from
Chisinau, Moldova.

I build backend systems with a product mindset: from the first architecture
decision to production reliability, team velocity, and business impact.

I am the **co-founder, development lead, and architect of
[Tabrica](https://tabrica.ru)**. My work there is not only about writing backend
code. I shape the product architecture, define technical direction, connect
business needs with engineering decisions, and keep the system ready for growth.

Alongside Tabriсa, I work at **[KTS](https://kts.tech)** and contribute as an
in-house developer to **[SmartBot Pro](https://smartbotpro.ru)**, one of the
largest bot builder platforms in Russia, including its service desk, widgets,
and support tooling.
